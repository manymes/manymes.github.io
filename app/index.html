How-To: Install manymes-chrome-extension

  1. unzip manymes-chrome-extension.zip
  2. open chrome://extensions/
  3. activate developer mode (checkbox)
  4. press load unpacked extensions...
  5. choose the unpacked manymes-crhome-extension folder
  6. activate browsing by clicking on the meaningfull icon in your toolbar
  7. enjoy.
